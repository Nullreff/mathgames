Ever wonder how fast you could get a matrix into reduced row echelon form? Well now you can practice! Use the simple commands below to modify the matrix to the right. Brackets [ ] indicate a key on the keyboard.

Use [r], [f] and [v] to select the different rows of the matrix.
Use [a] to add, [s] to subtract and [w] to swap. Select a row, hit [a], [s] or [w] and then select another row.
Use [x] to multiply and [d] to divide. Select a row, hit [x] or [d] then hit [2]-[9] to multiply or divide. Hit [1] to change the sign.
